Earn your Open Badge

Upon completion of this webinar, you have the opportunity to earn either a Gold, Silver, or Bronze Badge indicating your competence and familiarity with using Linked Data with MARC records. By registering and attending this webinar you automatically earned your Bronze Badge that you can receive by sending an email to Jeremy Nelson.

Earning your Silver or Gold Badge

You need to answer correctly 60% of the badge assessments in the presentation for a silver. To earn a Gold Badge you need to answer correctly 87% of the badge assessments.

Software Prerequisites

Please install iPython on your computer.
