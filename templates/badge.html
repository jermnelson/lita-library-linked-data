Earn your Open Badge

Upon completion of this webinar, you have the opportunity to earn either a Gold, Silver, or Bronze Badge indicating your competence and familiarity with using Linked Data with MARC records. By registering and attending this webinar you automatically earned your Bronze Badge that you can receive by registering here. If you wish to earn a Silver Badge, you need to answer correctly 60% of the badge assessments in the presentation. To earn a Gold Badge you need to answer correctly 87% of the badge assessments in the presentation.

Bronze Badge

The Bronze Badge is earned by attending the Coding Experiments with MARC and Linked Data in the LITA Linked Data Webinar Series.

Silver Badge

The Silver Badge is earned by scoring over 60% on the badge assessments.

Gold Badge

Earn your Gold Badge by scoring over 87% on the badge assessments.
